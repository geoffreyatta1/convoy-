Convoy keeps your whole group connected and on track — whether you're a family caravanning to a holiday destination, a car club heading to a meet, or friends road-tripping across the country together.

REAL-TIME CONVOY TRACKING
See every vehicle in your group on a live map. Know exactly who's ahead, who's behind, and how fast everyone is moving — without anyone having to text.

TURN-BY-TURN NAVIGATION FOR EVERY CAR
The convoy leader sets a destination and everyone gets turn-by-turn directions synced to the same route. Followers who join late automatically get a merge route to intercept the convoy — no confusion, no left behind.

APPLE CARPLAY SUPPORT
Keep your eyes on the road. The Convoy CarPlay interface shows the current maneuver, upcoming turn, convoy members and their speeds, and a push-to-talk button — all on your car's built-in display without touching your phone.

WALKIE-TALKIE PUSH-TO-TALK
Hold the Talk button and speak directly to the whole convoy — or privately to a single driver. Crystal-clear audio powered by Agora, the same technology used by professional live-streaming platforms.

GAP ALERTS
If a vehicle falls too far behind, every driver in the convoy gets an instant alert. Customise the gap distance in Settings so you decide what "too far" means for your group.

HAZARD REPORTING
Spot something on the road? Report it in one tap — police, accidents, debris, roadworks. Hazards are shared instantly with every convoy member so they can slow down or change lanes in time.

AI DRIVING ASSISTANT (Convenience & Roadtrip)
Ask the AI assistant anything hands-free — "How long until we arrive?", "Find a petrol station near us", "What's the traffic like ahead?" — and get spoken answers without looking at your screen.
